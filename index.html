Crystal Collector Game

How To Play The Game


  1. A random number will be given to you at the beginning of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Pink
    • Yellow
  3. When you click a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. If your total score matches the random number, you will win the game.
  5. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose the game.
  6. Each crystal's value is hidden until you click on it.
  7. The value of each crystal will change everytime the game resets.
